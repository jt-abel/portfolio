A compact DC motor assembly with an integrated magnetic encoder and standardized mounting interface, designed for use in closed-loop position and velocity control applications.

---

## Overview

The actuator has 6 pins total: 2 pins for the motor control, 2 pins for encoder power, and 2 pins for encoder I2C communication. This actuator simplifies the motor control by housing the encoder internally and providing ample mounting interfaces.

---

## Specifications

| Parameter          | Value                        |
|--------------------|------------------------------|
| Motor              | TT Motor                     |
| Encoder IC         | AS5600                       |
| Encoder Resolution | 12-bit (4096 CPR)           |
| Interface          | I2C (400kHz fast mode)       |
| Magnet             | 6mm diametric, axial field   |
| Supply voltage     | 6-12V motor / 3.3V encoder   |
| Mounting pattern   | 8x M2.5, xxmm bolt circle    |
| Output shaft       | [diameter, length, flat]     |
