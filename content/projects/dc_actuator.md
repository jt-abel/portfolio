# DC Actuator

A DC actuator with an internal encoder and circular mounting holes

---

## Project Summary

The actuator has 6 pins total: 2 pins for the motor control, 2 pins for encoder power, and 2 pins for encoder I2C communication. This actuator simplifies the motor control by housing the encoder internally and providing ample mounting interfaces.
